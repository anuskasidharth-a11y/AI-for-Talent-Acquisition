# Case Studies

This folder is reserved for future AI-driven Talent Acquisition case studies.

The current repository focuses on documenting AI-assisted recruitment workflows, methodologies, frameworks, and practical approaches used across different stages of the hiring process.

Future case studies added here will focus on real recruitment scenarios with measurable outcomes, such as:

- Hiring funnel analysis
- Sourcing strategy improvements
- Candidate engagement outcomes
- Recruitment process optimization
- AI workflow implementation results

At this stage, no artificial metrics or simulated business outcomes are included. Case studies will be added once they are supported by real project data, measurable results, or documented recruitment experiences.

The objective is to maintain transparency and ensure that portfolio examples represent genuine outcomes rather than assumed impact.
