Resume Screener

AI for Talent Acquisition · Live Tool

Paste one job description, add up to 10 candidate resumes, and get a ranked, explainable comparison. This tool looks for context and transferable experience, not just exact keywords — a missing exact term is flagged for validation, not scored to zero.

AI-generated scores are recommendations, not final hiring decisions. Recruiter validation is required before shortlisting. A candidate with a lower score can still be relevant if transferable experience exists — nothing here rejects a candidate automatically.
